Data Dunkers Introduction

Data Dunkers is a fun and interactive way to learn about data science, programming, and AI with basketball. Dell Technologies has partnered with the PS43 Foundation (founded by NBA Player Pascal Siakam) to help students learn foundational data science skills and AI-enabled analytics through basketball statistics in a fun and inclusive environment.

The PS43 Foundation is a not-for-profit organization that finds unique ways to make a difference in the lives of children through education. Through financial literacy, sports, and life skills our goal is to teach children and give them an advantage in society.
